EQSTART v^{veh} = M_{ned}^{veh} v^{ned} EQEND

EQSTART v^{ned} EQEND is a 3-element vector expressed in the NED frame.
EQSTART v^{veh} EQEND is the same 3-element vector expressed in the vehicle frame.
